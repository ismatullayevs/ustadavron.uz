Toshev Davron

1970 - born in the Bukhara oblast, Uzbekistan

1985-1989 - studied at the Fifth Polytechnic

1992-1994 - studied under the masters of the Sanoi Nafis artists' cooperative

1994-1997 - Head Artist at the small enterprise "Memor" in the town of Kagan (Bukhara oblast, Uzbekistan)

1997-2010 - eacher at the boarding school specialising in art, Bukhara, Uzbekistan

From 2000 - member of the Khunarmand Association

2001, 2003 - participated in the festival of artisans from Central Asian countries, Almata, Khazakstan

2005 - Participated in the Miniatures of Uzbekistan exhibitions, Paris, France

From 2006 - member of the Creative Union of artists.

2006 - participated in the "Marche` d'art de Noel" exhibitions, Thionville, France

2006 - personal exhibitions, Brussels, Belgium

2007 - personal exhibitions, Lyon, France

2008 - personal exhibitions, Grenoble, France

2009 - personal exhibitions, Lille, France

2011 - participated in the "Aquarelles" exhibition, Arpajon, France

2011 - awarded with a badge on the occasion of Uzbekistan's 20th anniversary

2012 - personal exhibition in the Kamoliddin Behzod Memorial

2013 - participated in "Week of culture" in Avaza national touristic zone. Avaza (Turkmenistan)

2013 - participated in the "Week of Fine Art"

2015 - participated in the "Mother in colours"

2015 - participated in the Festival dell'arte

2015 - participated in ARTS & CULTURE D'O UZBEKISTAN, Paris, France

2017 - Personal exhibitions, House of photos (Tashkent)

2017-2018 - Master of producing at Artist's college of the Republic of Uzbekistan

2018 - Founder and director of limited liablity company "Buxoro Ipak Qog'oz"

2019 - Winner of review-competition for the presidential award "Tashabbus 2019" in the direction of "The best craftsman of the year"

2019 - participated in the exhibition based on the programme "One place, One way", Huangzhou, China

2019 - Personal exhibition, Grenoble, France

2020 - personal exhibition in the Kamoliddin Behzod Memorial Park - Museum, Tashkent

2020 - awarded with the gold medal of the Academy of Art of the Republic of Uzbekistan

2021 - personal exhibition. Baselstrasse. Switzerland.

2021 - Winner in the nomination "The best teacher - student school of the year" TOP Art 2021
